Rice with Chicken

Description.

This recipe is a simple version of the classic chicken rice, since only the breast is used, thus achieving a delicious dish in a short time.

The secret is the crushed Alicante paprika, which enhances the flavors of the tomato and creates a sweet, smooth sauce with a slight spicy touch.

Ingredients:

  • One teaspons of oil
  • Two onions
  • One green pepper

Steps:

  1. Chop the onion and pepper.
  2. Place them in a pot with oil and saut√© them over low heat for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the diced chicken and cook for about 5 more minutes or until the chicken cubes are lightly browned.
