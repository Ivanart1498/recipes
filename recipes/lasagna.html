Lasagna's Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a traditional dish of Italian cuisine, more specifically from Bologna. It is made with thin layers of dough, interspersed with meat or vegetables and sauce, which can be white sauce, cheese sauce or red sauce. This recipe is a meat lasagna, so the use of Alicante Meat Seasoning is essential to enhance flavors and achieve a moist and tender filling.
